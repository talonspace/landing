The Problem

Frontier autonomy cannot depend on the cloud.

Most AI infrastructure assumes abundant compute, persistent connectivity, centralized observability, and a human operator nearby. Mission systems often have the opposite operating profile.

  • Intermittent communications and high latency
  • Limited power, compute, and thermal budgets
  • Safety requirements that demand predictable behavior
  • Operators who need audit trails, validation, and assurance evidence
